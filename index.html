RAIL Initiative Open License Generator

Responsible AI Licenses (RAIL) empower developers to restrict the use of their AI technology in order to prevent irresponsible and harmful applications. These licenses include behavioral-use clauses which grant permissions for specific use-cases and/or restrict certain use-cases. In case a license permits derivative works, RAIL Licenses also require that the use of any downstream derivatives (including use, modification, redistribution, repackaging) of the licensed artifact(s) must abide by the behavioral-use restrictions.

This tool is intended to allow for education on and experimentation with Responsible AI Licenses (RAILs), and is in ongoing development by the RAIL Initiative. Always consult an attorney before releasing artifacts with a license generated here. By using this tool, you confirm that:

  1. I, as the Licensor, have all rights to the licensed technology, code, or data.
  2. This is a one-way license from Licensor to Licensee without exchange of intellectual property.
  3. The party using the licensed artefact(s) agrees to the license terms prior to accessing the IP.
